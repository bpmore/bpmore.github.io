Je Suis Brent

I am currently the Director of Communication Services at Eastern New Mexico University.

For thirteen years, I worked as the Director of Web Development at the University of Central Arkansas. As director, I coordinated the overall web and new media presence at UCA. I also worked with various departments on campus to develop promotional websites that advanced the university’s mission to both internal and external audiences.

Involved with the implementation of the university’s “Graphic Identity Style Guide”, I was also part of the “Branding Team”.

As a former member of the District IV Board of Directors for CASE (Council for the Advancement and Support of Education), I am continually working to create synergy with all advancement efforts on campus and within the community. I believe all advancement areas are capable of creating a holistic environment to support students throughout their tenure at the university – and beyond.

I graduated from the University of Central Arkansas with a degree in Mass Communications. Most recently, I obtained my master’s degree from Webster University in Communications Management.
